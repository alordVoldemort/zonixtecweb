Frequently Asked Questions

What industries can benefit from Computer Vision?
Retail, healthcare, manufacturing, logistics, security, and many more. Computer vision has applications across virtually every industry that deals with visual data.
Do I need huge amounts of data to start with Computer Vision?
Not always—transfer learning and pre-trained models allow us to work efficiently with smaller datasets. We can leverage existing models and fine-tune them for your specific use case.
Can Computer Vision be integrated into mobile apps?
Yes, we develop lightweight models suitable for mobile and edge devices. Our solutions can run efficiently on smartphones and other resource-constrained environments.
How secure is facial recognition technology?
We implement strict security measures, anonymization, and compliance with global standards. Data privacy and security are paramount in all our computer vision solutions.
What is the timeline for developing a Computer Vision solution?
Typically ranges from 6–12 weeks, depending on project complexity and dataset size. We provide detailed timelines after our initial discovery phase.
