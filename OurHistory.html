OUR HISTORY

Zonixtec was founded in 2024 with a simple goal: to build digital solutions that help businesses work smarter. What began with five passionate developers in a small room has now grown into a fast-moving technology company offering CRM, ERP, HRMS, custom software, mobile apps, and cloud solutions.

From building our first CRM solution to expanding into ERP systems, custom software, mobile apps, and cloud services, every milestone has strengthened our mission—to create technology that simplifies operations and drives real business impact.
