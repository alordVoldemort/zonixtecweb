HRMS Solutions at Zonixtec

Empower People. Simplify Processes.

At Zonixtec, we build intelligent HRMS solutions that make workforce management effortless. From onboarding and payroll to performance tracking and attendance, our HRMS automates manual HR tasks, reduces paperwork, and provides real-time visibility into employee data—so your HR team can focus on people, not processes.

HR Management Solutions

Key HRMS Features

Employee Management System

Centralized dashboard for employee data, profiles, and history with role-based access control.

Payroll & Compensation Management

Automate salary calculations, tax deductions, and payslip generation with compliance-ready features.

Attendance & Leave Management

Track working hours, shifts, and leaves with biometric or web-based integration.

Recruitment & Onboarding

Manage job postings, applicant tracking, and smooth employee onboarding processes.

Performance & Appraisal Module

Set goals, monitor KPIs, and conduct transparent evaluations with analytics.

Cloud-Based Access

Remote HR operations with secure, encrypted storage and real-time analytics dashboards.
