Connecting Devotees with Digital Darshan

How a centuries-old temple embraced technology to reach devotees worldwide with Live Darshan, Online Donations, and eSevas

Client

Kaleshwari Mandir Annadan Chhatra Trust

Industry

Religious & Non-Profit

Primary Goal

Extend temple's reach beyond physical boundaries

Platform

Bilingual responsive website (English & Marathi)

The Client

They wanted to share accurate timings, rituals, and festival updates without confusion or dependency on word-of-mouth.

A historic temple with a strong offline following but limited digital presence.

They needed a simple platform that would keep younger devotees connected to the temple, no matter where they were.

Wanted a clean, devotional, and easy-to-use website—not a flashy corporate design.

Their vision was clear: make the divine accessible anytime, anywhere.

Pain Points

Why Us

Clarity

Information presented in a way that removes confusion and builds trust.

Simplicity

A seamless digital experience that anyone can use without struggle.

Tradition

Designed with respect for cultural values, heritage, and devotion.

Accessibility

Easy for every age group, every device, and every type of user.

  • Details on history, rituals, and eSevas
