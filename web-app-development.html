Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical timeline for developing a web application?
The timeline depends on the complexity and features of your web application. A simple application may take 4-8 weeks, while more complex enterprise solutions can take 3-6 months. We provide a detailed timeline after the initial discovery phase.
Do you provide post-development support and maintenance?
Yes, we offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages that include bug fixes, security updates, performance optimization, and feature enhancements to ensure your web application remains secure and up-to-date.
Can you integrate third-party APIs into my web application?
Absolutely. We have extensive experience integrating various third-party APIs including payment gateways, CRM systems, email services, social media platforms, and more to extend the functionality of your web application.
How do you ensure the security of web applications?
We follow security best practices throughout the development process, including secure coding standards, regular security testing, data encryption, authentication and authorization mechanisms, and compliance with industry security standards.
Can you migrate my existing web application to a new technology stack?
Yes, we specialize in application modernization and migration services. We can help migrate your legacy applications to modern, scalable technology stacks while preserving data integrity and business logic.
